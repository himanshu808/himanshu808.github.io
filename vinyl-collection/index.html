My Vinyl Collection

Rock music has always held a special place in my heart, and recently, I’ve embarked on an exciting new adventure in the world of vinyl records. My collection is still in its infancy, boasting just three records, but each one holds a special place in my heart. These vinyl albums have quickly become the focal point of my music library, with their tactile beauty and captivating album artwork.


While I’m currently without a record player, I see this as an opportunity to carefully research and select the perfect turntable to enhance my listening experience. The prospect of immersing myself in the unique world of vinyl, complete with its characteristic crackles and pops, fills me with anticipation.


In the coming months and years, I look forward to growing my vinyl collection, uncovering rare gems, and building an archive of rock music history on these iconic discs. It’s not just about the music, it’s about the act of discovering and the pure joy of experiencing rock’s timeless classics through the captivating medium of vinyl records.


Collection so far -

  • Abbey Road - The Beatles
  • American Idiot - Green Day
  • The Dark Side of the Moon - Pink Floyd
